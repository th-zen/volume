Rocking Rotting Minds

Thoughts carry weight, thoughts have volume, and if there are too many of them, your head feels like it’s going to explode. Overthinking is a common phenomenon, especially in young adults trying to figure everything out. Future and past, imposter syndrome at your job, relationships with friends and family, new people, new places, issues with your own body… The list of topics is endless. And while overthinking can turn dark pretty quickly, this magazine leans into the sarcastic, weird and oddly specific side of it.
