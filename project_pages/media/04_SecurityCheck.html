Security Check

A normal security check at an airport - but not all is as it seems. When a clown steps through the gate, the security guards notice his bag is suspiciously mysterious. Upon inspection, the bag surprises with just how much it can carry. An absurdist 2D animation playing with the idea of infinite volume inside a normal travel bag.
