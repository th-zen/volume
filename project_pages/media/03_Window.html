Window

A protagonist sits in an apartment in a post-pocalyptic city and passes the time listening to the radio and eating crackers. The room in which the person is sitting has a window, some light shines into the dusty room. On the radio, people talk about a dangerous parasite, killing people around the world. During boring activities, the protagonist is sick of staying inside. The desire for fresh air grows ever greater.
