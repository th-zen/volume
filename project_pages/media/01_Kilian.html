Kilian

This short film provides an insight into the merging of reality and the digital world and the challenges it brings with it: Ruben, a young student, wakes up as he often does in the late morning sun and immediately immerses himself in his digital world, dominated by notifications and social media. On his way through his everyday life, distracted by Tinder, Instagram and co. he encounters difficulties that frustrate him.
