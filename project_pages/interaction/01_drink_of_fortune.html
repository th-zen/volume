Drink of Fortune

Drink of Fortune is an Instagram Filter that can see your future: Your face is scanned, your fortune read, and your card revealed. The illustrated tarot card you are presented depicts one of six iconic cocktails and tells you which drink lies in your near future. For some added fun, the actual cards can be picked up and taken home or to your nearest bar. The backside holds the recipe of your drink, so nothing can stand between you and your fortune.
