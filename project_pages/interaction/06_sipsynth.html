Sipsynth

Embark on a sensory journey with our innovative cocktail robot, designed to revolutionize the bar and restaurant experience. At the heart of the concept lies the exploration of cocktails through sound. Using a MIDI Sprout biodata sonification sensor, we capture the unique sounds of each cocktail ingredient. These harmonious notes are then translated into an auditory symphony.
